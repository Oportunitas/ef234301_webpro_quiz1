The foods of Palembang

-Palembang is renowned for its unique and flavorful cuisine, which features a variety of dishes that showcase the city's rich culinary heritage-

Pempek

These are fish cakes made from ground fish and starch, typically served with a sweet and tangy vinegar-based sauce. Pempek comes in various shapes and sizes, with the most common being kapal selam (literally "submarine"), which has an egg inside.

Tekwan

These are fish cakes made from ground fish and starch, typically served with a sweet and tangy vinegar-based sauce. Pempek comes in various shapes and sizes, with the most common being kapal selam (literally "submarine"), which has an egg inside.

Laksan

A type of rice noodle dish served with a coconut milk-based broth and various toppings, such as shredded chicken, shrimp, and bean sprouts.

Mie Celor

A dish featuring yellow noodles served with a rich, creamy shrimp and coconut milk sauce, along with boiled eggs and prawns.

Pindang Musi

A tangy and spicy fish soup made with tamarind, chili, and various spices, often served with rice.
