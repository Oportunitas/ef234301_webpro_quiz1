Palembang is a city located on the island of Sumatra. It is the capital of the South Sumatra province and is known for its rich cultural heritage, traditional cuisine, and as a center for the production of songket textiles. The city is also famous for its connection to the Musi River and its iconic Ampera Bridge.

I was born in Palembang and spent a significant part of my childhood there. Palembang was relatively moderately developed when I was born, but today it's among the most developed in Indonesia.
