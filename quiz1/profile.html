Taib Izzat Samawi

"Destitutus ventis, remos adhibe."

-In destitute of winds, take thy oars-

Raised to solve puzzles and riddles as a child, problem solving have now become an obsession for me. Being able to figure out how the world works, from the small things unnoticed to the grand endeavors renowned, have always been an energizer and my fuel for happiness.

Born in Palembang to a mixed heritage, I was raised in a family and community of diversity, who have tought me humility, compassion, and strength in heterogeneity.
